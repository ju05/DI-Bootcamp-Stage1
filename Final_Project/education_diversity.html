AI can mean many different things. Even experts have a hard time agreeing on a single definition for AI. At AI4ALL, we think about AI as a branch of computer science that allows computers to make predictions and decisions to solve problems. When you hear that AI is being used in a product, that usually refers to an area of AI called machine learning. Machine learning (ML) is an approach to teach computers how to make decisions and predictions, by giving them the ability to learn from data.
